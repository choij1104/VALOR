The model

Two entities, cleanly separated.

A charitable arm holds donor trust; a technology arm builds and licenses the platform — connected by an arm's-length contract, never by donor funds.

NONPROFIT · 501(c)(3)

VALOR Foundation

Receives tax-deductible donations, verifies veterans, disburses medical aid, owns the brand and public trust.

ARM'S-LENGTH SERVICES CONTRACT
FOR-PROFIT · LLC

VALOR Tech LLC

Builds the software and verification system; licenses the platform to other veteran and community organizations.

Our team

Who's behind VALOR.

Two co-founders pairing mission and medicine — building a fund veterans and donors can trust, with the governance to keep it accountable.

PN

Dr. Patrick Ng, MD

CO-FOUNDER & MEDICAL DIRECTOR

Emergency medicine physician and medical toxicologist. U.S. Air Force veteran with combat deployment experience and former director of an en route care research center. Brings frontline military medicine and trauma expertise to VALOR's verification and care standards.

JC

Dr. Jae Hyek Choi

CO-FOUNDER & EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

MSc, PhD, DVSc. A trauma toxicology researcher with over two decades in combat casualty care and preclinical research, and author of numerous peer-reviewed publications. Leads VALOR's mission, platform, and day-to-day operations.

+Advised by Jaehyun Yang, CPA (financial controls) and a majority-independent board.
