Tax & transparency

Why a registered fund? A 501(c)(3) can receive tax-deductible gifts, hold them accountably, and disburse for documented medical costs — three things a personal fundraiser generally cannot.

For donors: gifts are deductible to the extent allowed by law, with an automatic itemized receipt. For veterans: charitable medical aid is disbursed for documented costs and is generally not taxable income.

Every recipient is service-verified via ID.me or DD-214 review, and spending is tracked to documented care.
